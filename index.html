Recycling Information and Awareness Points

    Waste Recycling:

  • Types of Recyclables: Paper, cardboard, glass, plastics, metals, electronics, organic waste.
  • Recycling Steps: Collection, sorting, cleaning, processing, manufacturing, purchasing recycled products.
  • Benefits: Conserves resources, saves energy, reduces pollution, decreases landfill use.

    Awareness Points:

  • Reduce and Reuse: Prioritize reducing waste and reusing items.
  • Proper Sorting: Ensure recyclables are correctly sorted.
  • E-Waste Recycling: Properly dispose of electronics.
